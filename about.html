Support

Premium support for Classy is available to companies and individuals on an hourly basis.
Get in touch here for more details.

Classy's core contributors are available to help you with your iOS project, including:

  • Help you to style complex view components
  • Converting an existing app to use Classy
  • Troubleshooting issues with Classy
  • Optimizing view rendering and application performance
  • Checking for Classy best practices

Contributing

Get involved with Classy by opening an issue or submitting a pull request. Please read our contributing guidelines beforehand.
